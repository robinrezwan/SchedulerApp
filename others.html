Result Calculation Sheet

Use this Excel spreadsheet to calculate your CGPA and store the marks and GPAs for all the semesters. You just have to enter the marks. The rest will be done automatically. For Clearance/Carry Over/Improvement, you must choose the examination type from the drop-down menu in the Examination column.

Once you're done entering the marks, simply save the file to store your result. You can input and store your marks and see your GPA and CGPA for all the semesters in the same way.
